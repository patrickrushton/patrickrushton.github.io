Goed Bezig

Client
Arbo Unie
Platform
Native iOS & Android app
Role
Interaction & Visual Design

Goed Bezig is a ten week programme that improves performances of teams. A team is brought together under guidance from one of Arbo Unieâ€™s coaches who leads group discussions as well as individual assignments. The aim is improve energy, self awareness and flexibility towards change.

Each team member installs the app which is linked to the team-specific programme with assignments and content chosen by the coach. The users complete a daily reflection which over time provides an insight into what gives them energy, and what takes it away. The app also facilitates assignments in four categories: perspective, autonomy, connection and growth.

Interaction & visual design

After helping to facilitate a creative workshop with the client, I was commissioned to work on-site with the client's own development team. We took a SCRUM approach which enabled us to launch the app quickly. The visual style was loosley based on Arbo Unie's house style with influence from Google's Material guidelines. I worked closely with the developers to take into account differing screen sizes and implementations across iOS and Android devices.
